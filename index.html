FAQs

Can I get selected to multiple projects?

NO, a person can become a mentee or a mentor only for a project at a time.

What is a role of a mentor and how do they assist?

A mentor is a slected student of NITK based on his/her skills and passion, who would be able to provide guidance, support, and expertise to ensure you have a fulfilling learning experience.

Is there any registration fee?

NO, there is no registeration fees for participation.

How will I know I am selected?

The applicants will be notified by their mentors through their provided email.
